Collections

This collection is a love letter to color and movement.

The droplets ascend along the piece, passing through a gradient that ranges from intense reds to vibrant oranges and turquoises, inspired by the flow of hot wax in a lava lamp.

In this series, lines run through the piece, with colorfull patterns that enchance its shape, making each one unique and unrepeatable.

Inspired by bead jewelry made as protection or representations of power and wisdom, the necklaces are symbols that tell the stories of culture and civilizations.

Mountain Drops is a collection of unique pieces, each sculpted to resembling a rocky mountain.

Each piece represents a small landscape encapsulated within a drop-shaped vase, capturing the very essence of a mountainous fragment and evoking the natural gestures of the kurinuki technique

Caras Familiares explores the memory and images that we keep of each person that we have met.

Each piece has a cut that leaves exposed faces, this modification shows us a window, and it let us see our memories.

Crafting Stories in Clay

Meet Laura Melissa , a talented ceramist whose art bridges cultures and traditions. Born in Colombia and now calling Mexico home, Laura draws inspiration from the vibrant landscapes, rich histories, and diverse textures of both countries. Each piece she creates is a reflection of her journey, blending her Colombian roots with the influences of her adopted home.
